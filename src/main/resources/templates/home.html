What is Tech{knowledge}y?

Tech{knowledge}y is the culmination of an 18-week coding journey. A journey fraught with frustration and "ah-ha" moments. As students our greatest asset is the knowledge that others possess and one of man's greatest gifts is the passing of the torch from one generation to the next. As we stepped into this new world we found ourselves inundated with information and help, but the cohesiveness of the message was lost to us. After struggling to find the sort of tech knowledge we sought, grasping the information we've learned, we've lit our own torch that we're proud to pass onto those after us. We are helping put the knowledge back into tech.

We are Tech{knowledge}y
